Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 3, 2025

The Veyaaura App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app to upload content to Pinterest.

1. Information We Collect

We only access the information necessary to perform app functions, including:

  • Access tokens provided by Pinterest to authorize the uploading of images to your boards.
  • Basic account details required to associate the uploaded content with your Pinterest account.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Upload images to boards you authorize on Pinterest.
  • Store access tokens securely to allow future uploads without reauthorization.
  • Ensure app functionality and provide technical support if needed.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. All tokens and data are stored securely and used only for app operations.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely in our backend.

5. User Consent

By authorizing the Veyaaura App with your Pinterest account, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@veyaaura.com.
